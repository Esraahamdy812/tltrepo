Villa Coconut

October 07    th, 2021

Villa Coconut has a treat for every mood. This week, especially, will be like no other where every night is the night to be at Villa Coconut with our DJs and their lively beats.

Mondays: Unwind with us for after-work drinks every Monday from 7 PM to 10 PM. Ask your waiter for what we have prepared for your after-work remedy.

Tuesdays: Join us every Tuesday for Tequila Night with our designated Tequila menu and an indulgence of 3-for-2 tequila shots during our Early Dinner slot (7 PM to 10 PM).

Fridays: Enjoy our Friday cocktails under the sun from 12 PM to 6 PM. Bring your bottle and let us create iconic drinks designed for your utmost indulgence.

Saturdays:“Meet Me Downtown” every Saturday from 3 PM to 7 PM for a special offering alongside funky tunes. This month you’re meeting us for Sangrias, where one jug is on you, and another is on us.

For more information, please visit our Instagram:@villa.coconut
