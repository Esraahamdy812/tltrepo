SWEET INTIMACY

It is a home outside of home, in every detail, where through every corner, you'll find familiarity. Villa Coconut is beautifully set by the azure waters of El Gouna Red Sea; bringing you the best of what the season can offer; from a carefully tailored menu of delicacies to home- like peacefulness and exquisite indulgence.

A PLACE OF LOVE

Villa Coconut is our new home, built on the essence of comfort and originality. Inspired by the most versatile fruit, the coconut. This concept is rough on the outside yet soft from the inside, textured yet smooth and mostly adorned in white, reflecting on the identity of El Gouna Red Sea. Celebrating refined hospitality and tranquil experiences, we present to you - Villa Coconut.
