AN ORIGIN STORY

Originating from the very essence of life, that is, culture. It is embedded within our hearts, lives and families, an ethereal connection that lived long before us. We are deeply connected with our roots, hence this explains the many iconic traditions we simply live by until today The Origins draws its inspiration from the Mediterranean cultures and communities. From great Greece and Rome to Ancient Egypt and Mesopotamia and even to the frontlines of Spain and Morocco.

TOGETHERNESS

An outdoor restaurant and bar with an indoor partition, all of which are meticulously designed with passion, delicacy and inspiration. It is the perfect spot for families and friends to get away from the bustling city and enjoy an authentic-inspired cuisine in comfortable surroundings. It is an open space of beauty, encouraging families to gather for a lovely day in the sun. From the moming up until nightfall, The Origins will offer breakfast, lunch and dinner; an extensive menu of sweet and savory delicacies that will suit the distinctive tastes of our dientele. We are reinforcing the notion of togetherness'; a family value that we cherish so deeply in our hearts.
