BEAUTY IN SIMPLICITY

Finding new roots within the beauty of the Mediterranean, The Lemon Tree & Co. Hacienda White is grounded by nature, fed by art and is hosted by The Lemon Tree family at the beautiful Hacienda White in the North Coast. The venue embraces the nature of simplicity through an all- white raw interior, a fine selection of subtle greenery, hand-crafted accents and direct views to the Mediterranean Sea.

DIARY OF FLAVOURS

Guests have the luxury of indulging in sumptuous delicacies offered by our exquisite menu while enjoying the Mediterranean fresh breeze as the timeless sky changes in colour. The Lemon Tree & Co. Hacienda White is also known for its signature Tuesday nights - "Wine & Dine' with Harfoush; a dazzling night to wine, dine and dance to the tunes of our golden age. The Lemon Tree & Co Hacienda White is a soothing sanctuary by the sea, an open space of beauty and beholds nature at its core.
