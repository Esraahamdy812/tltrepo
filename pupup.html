  • Spring 2014;
    The Beginning

    The Lemon Tree & Co. Imperial Boat Zamalek

    Once upon a tree, a magic seed was planted in the midst of Egypt's chaos in 2014. It was the time where hospitality suffered the post- revolution. Nevertheless, it was the beginning of something new and bright; The Lemon Tree & Co. Zamalek Restaurant and Bar rose tall in Cairo with a magnificent Nile view. Although many people thought that The Lemon Tree & Co. was a franchise obtained from abroad; we took pride in starting our very own fully Egyptian brand in the fine hospitality industry.

    ×

  • Summer 2014;
    A New Chapter

    The Beach Bar - The Lemon Tree & Co. Telal

    Starting a new Summer season, The Lemon Tree & Co introduced its first Summer Venue at the North Coast - The Beach Bar by The Lemon Tree & Co. It was the go-to, classy summer spot located at Telal. It was known best for its soothing Mediterranean ambiance, its extensive seasonal menu and Its uplifting music, all whilst overlooking a striking beach view.

    ×

  • Summer 2014;
    Where Magic Happened

    The Lemon Tree & Co. Marassi

    Making Summer a lot more exciting, we had opened the doors of The Lemon Tree & Co. Marassi, which brought together the best of hospitality and entertainment. It was known for its spectacular events during the weekends and its sumptuous Mediterranean cuisine all summer round. From then on, The Lemon Tree & Co. Marasst had become the most favoured nightlife experience at the North Coast, offering our guests a 'Family & Friends' party every Saturday night. It was the time and place where guests engaged their every sense without a moment of dullness.

    ×

  • Fall 2014;
    Somewhere Only We Know

    Club Clandestine

    Club Clandestine was a concept created to take our guests on an exclusive surprise dinner or party. Combining mystery and extravagance, Club clandestine carefully plans everything beforehand in secrecy and style; from the invitation and the transportation, to delivering a series of extraordinary experiences.

    ×

  • Summer 2015;
    A Connection was found

    Family & Friends

    It all started with our beloved friends and family; where we would unite together for the love of music. Talented artists from all around the world would produce and exclusively share their music with us, hence making this brand come to life. The concept of 'Family & Friends' was introduced at The Lemon Tree & Co. Marassi by creating remarkable experiences, where nights turned into mornings and friends turned into family. It has become an ever-growing nightlife brand, offering distinctive music and ever-lasting energy.

    ×

  • Summer 2015;
    Retail

    The Boutique

    Our expert team of artisans had created an array of products, including home wear, décor, apparel and more. In collaboration with a number of local designers, we decided to open a boutique store at the North Coast to offer our guests a specialized shopping experience that would suit their all-time summer tastes.

    ×

  • Spring 2016;
    A Transitional Season

    Beetroot by The Lemon Tree & Co.

    Beetroot by The Lemon Tree & Co. only lasted for one season; it was an oriental-inspired restaurant located by the Nile, offering its guests a rich menu of delicacies. It was a season of transition for The Lemon Tree & Co., a moment where the tree had decided to migrate somewhere else, grounding its roots at The Lemon Tree & Co. Katameya and ending its journey at The Lemon Tree & Co. Zamalek. This is what we call a healthy transition, a new beginning and yet another milestone of our ever-growing tree.

    ×

  • Fall 2016;
    A Place to Call Home

    The Lemon Tree & Co. Katameya

    With passion and ambition, a beautiful seed was planted, creating what is now The Lemon Tree & Co. Katameya, our most iconic restaurant and bar, located in Cairo. It is best known for its refined experiences and Its exquisite seasonal-changing menu of delicacies. This place has become a home for us; from its comfortable surroundings and cultivated dining to the great company of our dedicated teams and beloved guests.

    ×

  • Spring 2017;
    An Idea Comes to Life

    TLT Events & Entertainment

    Our team started creating events for TLT Concepts, for guests' social celebrations as well as for corporate renowned entities. This derived from the need of creativity, where merely an inspiring idea was developed to create a remarkable event. TLT Events & Entertainment has grown to become one of the main pillars of our tree, offering the service of event-management and execution and built on the foundation of inspiration and creativity.

  • Summer 2018;
    A Step into the Wild

    Rituals Beach Club & Restaurant

    "Behind carved doors and rustic walls, lives a magic place by the sea, that speaks of art and poetry, louder than words will ever be." Rituals Restaurant & Beach Club was created with the concept of wilderness, playfulness and originality. It is a bohemian-inspired private oasis offering guests a mix of sea, sand and sun, whilst engaging in a series of magnificent activities throughout the day. It has become the ideal spot for a great beach party on the weekends and an ultimate relaxation mood during weekdays.

    ×

  • Summer 2018;
    A Work of Art

    Can Limón

    Way before the summer of 2018, we had already started creating our very own array of décor items and elegant furnishings for our Restaurants, Bars and Beach clubs. All our pieces and accents were hand-crafted and designed by our talented team of artisans. Our pieces and décor left our guests in awe, hence raising a lot of questions of where they're from and if they can be purchased. Since then, the brand has evolved; becoming 'Can Limón'. Our very first concept store is located at Rituals in the North Coast; although the brand is now evolving regionally and globally; where Can Limon will soon open its doors at El Gouna, Cairo, Greece and Ibiza Town.

    ×

  • Summer 2019;
    Finding New Roots

    The Lemon Tree & Co. Hacienda White

    Finding new roots within the beauty of the Mediterranean, The Lemon Tree & Co. Hacienda White is grounded by nature, fed by art and is hosted by The Lemon Tree family at the beautiful Hacienda White in the North Coast. The venue embraces the nature of simplicity through an all-white raw Interior, a fine selection of subtle greenery, hand-crafted accents and direct views to the Mediterranean Sea. Our extensive menu is created to suit the refined tastes of our selected clientele. During the summer of 2019, our "Wine & Dine" Tuesday night events with Harfoush were the talk of the town!

  • 2019;
    An Authentic Story Unfolds

    Le Sidi Boutique Hotel & Restaurant

    Delving deeper into the high-end hospitality field, we decided to Introduce Le Sidi Boutique Hotel & Restaurant to the market and proudly open its doors during the Summer of 2019. It is a rich, authentic and welcoming sanctuary offering a locally- inspired experience on the beautiful ivory sands of Hacienda Bay, Sidi-Abdelrahman, North Coast. From its beautiful lagoon, swimming pool and private beach to its unique interior and personalized service, guests Indulge in hints and stories at every turn.

  • Summer 2019;
    A Place to Live

    TTLT Living

    A natural extension to our lifestyle; TLT Living offers our guests a variety of coastal residences across the Mediterranean in Egypt. "Rituals Village' in the North Coast is the first brand to be introduced in the real estate sector of TLT Concepts; the evolution of 'Rituals Beach Club & Restaurant'. We are expanding further, soon shedding light on 'Le Sidi Cabanas' and 'The Lemon Tree & Co. Boutique Hotel & Residences'. It is as if awning a soulful home by TLT with its furnishings, décor, ambiance and design.

  • 2020;
    A Home outside of Home

    Villa Coconut - El Gouna

    Another natural evolution of TLT Concepts extending into a more intimate and personalized-driven brand. Villa Coconut is our new home, built on the essence of comfort and homelike peacefulness, Inspired by the most versatile fruit, the coconut. This new conceptis rough on the outside yet soft from the inside, textured yet smooth and mostly adorned in white, reflecting on the identity of El Gouna. It's a home outside of home in every detail, where through every corner, you'll find familiarity. Celebrating refined hospitality, culinary expertise and tranquil experiences, we present to you - Villa Coconut.
