AUTHENTIC LIVING

A project by Palm Hills Developments & TLT Living, Le Sidi Cabanas are fully furnished, hotel- serviced homes, specifically designed with utmost care and elegance, offering an authentic and comfortable living experience. Extending from Le Sidi Boutique Hotel & Restaurant, Le Sidi Cabanas are located on the coast of Hacienda Bay at the North Coast in Egypt.

ROOTED IN NATURE

By swaying soft tones of our serene and beautiful earth, our design aesthetic is built on the concept of minimalism and authenticity. Exteriors consist of beautifully designed arches and earthly textures, evoking a feeling a soothing Mediterranean sanctuary. Le Sidi Cabanas are homes of which are elegantly furnished with raw authentic pieces to suit those who appreciate and admire art. The inspiration of culture intertwined with nature sets the mood for sim and sophisticated lifestyle.
