A PLACE TO CALL HOME

At Cairo's finest address is The Lemon Tree & Co. Katameya, serves as an exquisite fine dining experience filled with alluring surprises. We intend to remain true to our original rustic through embracing international cuisine by remaining loyal to great quality ingredients and bringing what's creative and delicious to the table. The Lemon Tree & Co. embraces an elegant interior reflecting upon the Earth's beauty through raw wood, subtle greenery and iconic locally made pieces of furniture for your comfort and indulgence.

EXCLUSIVE EXPERIENCE

As the day transcends into night, the ambiance becomes rich with warmth; lights become dimmed and our iconic tree is lit up above the bar. On Mondays, we celebrate the beauty of Live Music performances through our Wine & Dine concept, bringing you joy while you wine and dine to your favourite tunes of all time. On weekends, music gets uder and guests are swept off their feet to the bar for some cocktails and a long night of dancing.
