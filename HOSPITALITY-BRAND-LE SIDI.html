A LOCAL SANCTUARY

Prestigiously located in the heart of Sidi Abd el Rahman; Le Sidi Boutique Hotel & Restaurant is an authentic, rich and welcoming sanctuary offering a locally-inspired experience on the beautiful ivory sands of Hacienda Bay in the North Coast of Egypt.

EARTHY DELIGHT

Inspired by the notion of honouring our authentic culture and its abundant hospitality, Le Sidi beholds many culture-driven qualities. From its interior décor and earthy elements to its impeccable personalized service and soulful experiences; guests will indulge in hints and stories at every turn.

THE ULTIMATE HAVEN

On the golden coast of the Mediterranean, Le Sidi rooms, suites and villas are meticulously designed and fully furnished with soft earthy tones and accents to suit those who appreciate and admire art. All guest-rooms include a private terrace facing the lagoon, access to the pool and Le Sidi private beach.
Le Sidi Boutique Hotel & Restaurant is the ultimate summer destination for a family getaway, a couple retreat or even for tranquil solitude.
