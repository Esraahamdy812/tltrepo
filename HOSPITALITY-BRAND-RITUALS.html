SUN, SEA AND SAND

When we think of Rituals, we think of a perfect summer getaway, a staggering blue sea view and a beautiful golden sunset. Rituals is a bohemian- inspired private beach club in the North Coast, offering guests a chance to enjoy a perfect mix of sun, sea and sand. On weekdays, it's one of the most favoured places for a relaxing beach day for families, friends and couples; whereas on the weekend it's a party for the ones who love to dance.
Your Rituals are a series of activities during your Summer days, and for that reason, we create an experience suitable for each time of day. From sinking your feet in white sands and unwinding by the beach to munching on delicious bites and dancing the night away.

RITUALS EVENTS

our Summer season just got a lot more exciting! A series of enticing events take place at Rituals; from our very own iconic events of beach parties to exclusive social celebrations, weddings and more.
