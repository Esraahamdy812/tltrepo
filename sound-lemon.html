FOR THE LOVE OF MUSIC

The seed of music has profoundly grown over the years at The Lemon Tree & Co; a universal language of which we all love and comprehend. Our entertainment sector brings together talented artists from across the globe to share their music to the world and create a connection like no other.

We take pride in becoming a big part of the music community in Egypt, continuously adding to it and creating something that is quite extraordinary for the soul.

THE SEED OF IT ALL

The Sound of The Lemon Tree is a music concept that was created on Soundcloud by the talented music artist - Alex Kentucky. All the way from Ibiza, Kentucky is both a music partner and a dear friend to The Lemon Tree Team. He is known for his uplifting tunes and everlasting love for music. Music sets are specifically produced for TLT Concepts and wholeheartedly played at all of TLT events, restaurants and bars.

THE SEED OF IT ALL

The Sound of The Lemon Tree is a music concept that was created on Soundcloud by the talented music artist - Alex Kentucky. “

"The Sound Of The Lemon Tree" is like a child to me, after many years of collecting music I wanted to create a concept in which lounge and vocal deep house were the protagonists. Elegant music to have a drink or relax to at home. Timeless music in which I combine new trends with classics from my record case. All of this alongside the opportunity and the amazing work that my family from the "Lemon Tree" has done, have made it possible to publish more than 100 podcasts with millions of plays on Apple Music.

ALEX KENTUCKY
MUSIC CURATOR AT THE LEMON TREE & CO. GROUP
