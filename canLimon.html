ARTFUL LIVING

As TLT Concepts developed through the years, guests and regulars grew fond of every new outlet's unique interiors and raw surroundings. From there, the idea of Can Limón stemmed; a lifestyle concept store, capturing the very essence of the brand's aesthetic. It is a destination where shoppers can select their favorite pieces and forever keep a part of TLT'S ambiance in their homes.

DIVERSE PURPOSE

Can Limón is home, a wardrobe and a heartening experience altogether. A Lifestyle Concept Store empowering and supporting talented craftsmanship, while making the customer's journey unique and personalized. Can Limón coherently brings together all lifestyle elements under one roof. It is a place where artists meet creators venturing high quality, creative local and international products, showcasing a refined selection of apparel, accessories, furniture, décor and natural beauty products, and more.

INSPIRED SPACES

Can Limón embodies the identity of a traveler, moving from a place to another, to reveal its identity to the world. The project was homegrown in 2018, over a 200sqm space at the North Coast. A few years later, Can Limón opened its doors in Gouna, at Villa Coconut. 2021 marks the store's arrival to the city, opening a pop-up concept at Garden & Mall, in New Cairo. Future plans include an international expansion, to share the unique experience in other inspiring cities, all whilst shedding light on more artisans along the way.

CREATIVE ALLIANCES

Valuing the power of collaboration, the team behind Can Limòn aim partner up with talented artists to bring a different angle to the brand. Previous collaborations include a product photoshoot with world-renowned concept photographer and Stylist, Paulina Arcklin, which showcased the display in its most natural and creative form. Can Limòn is proud to recognize talents, and share the delightful outcome with world.
